About Me

I am an active student of Politeknik Negeri Medan, I made this website to fulfill the final project of English conversation 2 course.

I am your go-to online library for English books. My goal is to make reading easy and fun by offering a wide range of books for all ages and interests. Whether you love fiction, non-fiction, or academic books, we have something for you. Our collection includes classics, new releases, and everything in between. I believe in the joy of reading and strive to provide a seamless experience for our users. Browse, discover, and enjoy books from the comfort of your home. Happy reading!
